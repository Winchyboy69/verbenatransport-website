What We Do

About Us

BC's Trusted Heavy Equipment Carrier

Verbena Transport Ltd. is a BC-based heavy equipment transportation company built on a simple promise: show up on time, haul it safely, and treat every load like it's our own.

We've earned the trust of contractors, excavation companies, and construction crews across the province by delivering reliable, damage-free moves — whether it's a single machine across town or a full fleet across the province.

Fully licensed, insured, and permitted for oversize loads in British Columbia, Verbena Transport is the carrier contractors call when it has to go right.
